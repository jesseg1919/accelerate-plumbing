Meet the owner

I started Accelerate because I couldn't stand watching corners get cut.

I'm Landon — owner, lead technician, and the person who'll most likely be standing in your mechanical room. I built this company around a simple idea: premium work should function flawlessly and look like a professional did it. Tidy pipe runs, level fixtures, clean connections. Details most people never think about, but always notice once they're done right.

Before Accelerate, I saw too many jobs handed off to whoever was free, finished in a hurry, and left half-explained. I wanted to do it the other way — show up, take pride in it, and stand behind every install long after the invoice is paid.

So when you call Accelerate, you get me. I quote the job, I do the work, and my name is on the result. That's not a marketing line — it's the whole company.
